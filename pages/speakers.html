Akshita Mudgal

Indian Television Actress
11 AM

Akshita Mudgal is an Indian television actress who gained popularity for her excellent performance in the serial on SabTV named “Bhakarwadi”. Besides that, she has acted in a Kannada film “Ugramm”. Akshita shot a brief sequence in the movie “Mr. X”, in which she was rewarded with a chance to work alongside Emraan Hashmi. She has featured in more than 50 episodes of Crime Patrol and quite a few in CID as well. She has also starred in “Zindagi Wins” (2015), “Moh Ke Dhaage” (2017) and “Mitegi Laxman Rekha” (2018). She played the role of Reva in the serial “Half Marriage”. Akshita was recently seen in a short film “EXIT” that has more than 4.5 million views on YouTube as of today. After debuting as a participant in the reality dance show “Dance India Dance Lil Masters 2”, Akshita has never looked back. Today, this talent has bagged a lead role in the Sony Television Prime show “Ishk Par Zor Nahi”, which is very well appreciated by the audience across the nation. Besides acting, she is also an affluent dancer and has performed professionally in various shows and events.

Karan Sawhney

Professional Football Player
11 AM

Karan Sawhney is an Ex Professional football player in the Indian Super League and English Expert on Star Sports for the Indian Super league. He was also the Commentator on Star Sports for World Cup qualifiers, Asian Champions league & Indian Super League. With a vision to make people fit virtually, he Co-Founded 'The Tribe India' that offers a variety of top-notch workouts; tailor-made nutrition and emphasis on visualization and gratitude, all under the comfort of your home.

Cel Amada

Result Strategist
11 AM

Cel Amade is a Results Strategist with a track record of successfully facilitating and guiding University students, graduates, and young professionals in challenging times to recognize their strengths and take inspired action to continue achieving outstanding results. She is committed to helping young adults feel empowered to design their own world. Cel has delivered in-person and virtual workshops in over 25 countries and amassed over +525,000 views on YouTube in the last year. Her strengths as a Results Strategist have allowed her to collaborate with UK Higher Educators, BAME Ambassadors, Graduate Employers, Leaders and CEOs from the property, automotive, and fashion retail industries; international associations including Enterprise Nation & more.
